Privacy Policy for Browser Diary Extension

Last Updated: 16th July 2025

Overview

Browser Diary is a Chrome extension that helps users track their daily web browsing activity. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

Browser Diary collects the following information:

  • Website URLs you visit
  • Time spent on each website
  • Number of visits to each website
  • Automatic categorization of websites (Work, Social Media, Entertainment, etc.)

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Your browsing data never leaves your computer

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Display your daily browsing activity summary
  • Provide time tracking insights
  • Enable CSV export functionality for your personal use

Data Retention

  • Data is automatically cleared daily at midnight in your local timezone
  • You can manually clear data at any time by removing the extension

Third-Party Services

  • Icons provided by Icons8 (icons8.com)
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertisements are displayed

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • All data remains on your device
  • You can export your data via CSV
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all data

Changes to This Policy
